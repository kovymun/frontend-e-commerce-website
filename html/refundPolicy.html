Refund & Exchange Policy

At The Spice Pantry, we strive to ensure your satisfaction with every purchase. If for any reason you are not entirely satisfied with your order, we are here to help with our straightforward refund and exchange policy.

Returns:
  1. 14-Day Return Policy: You have 14 calendar days from the date of purchase to return an item for a refund or exchange.
  2. Condition of Items: Returned items must be in good condition and reasonably intact, considering the nature of the product. While we understand that you may have used a portion of the spice, we kindly request that the majority of the product remains unused. Additionally, please ensure that the packaging is retained in its original state to the best of your ability. This includes sealing the packaging securely to prevent spillage or contamination.
  3. Proof of Purchase:To be eligible for a return, you must provide a valid proof of purchase, such as a receipt or order confirmation email.
  4. Refund Process: Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you a notification through your preferred contact method regarding the status of your refund. If approved, the refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment within 7 days, depending on your card issuer's policies.
Exchanges:
  1. Product Exchange: We are happy to exchange items if you received a defective or damaged product, or if you simply wish to exchange it for a different variant.
  2. Process: To initiate an exchange, please contact our customer service team with your order details and the reason for exchange. Once approved, we will provide further instructions for returning the item.
Contact Us:
  1. If you have any questions or concerns regarding our refund and exchange policy, please don't hesitate to contact our customer service team customercare@thespicepantry.co.za. We are here to assist you and ensure your shopping experience with The Spice Pantry is enjoyable and hassle-free.
